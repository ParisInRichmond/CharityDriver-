Charity Driver is an AI-integrated Service that funnels resources to the homeless. We help donors funnel resources to the homeless by building a technical tool that connects donors with the homeless community.

About Us

At Charity Driver, our technology empowers users to do more than just donate it enables them to be catalysts for change. By collecting data on homeless demographics and psychographics, identifying specific needs, and fostering collaboration through private circles, we're not just addressing homelessness; we're revolutionizing how communities come together to create lasting impact. Through our platform, users can also assist charity foundations in pinpointing large concentrations of occupation in specific areas, ensuring that resources are directed where they're needed most. Join us in our mission to drive change, one empowered act at a time
